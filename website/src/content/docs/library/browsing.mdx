---
title: Browsing
description: Navigate your music library
---

bae provides several ways to explore your library.

## Views

### Albums

The default view shows all albums as a grid of cover art. Click an album to see its tracks.

### Artists

Browse by artist. Each artist shows their albums and tracks.

### Genres

Filter your library by genre. Genres are pulled from your releases' metadata.

## Search

The search bar finds albums, artists, and tracks instantly. Results appear as you type.

## Filtering

Combine multiple filters:

- **Year** - Filter by release year or decade
- **Format** - Filter by audio format (FLAC, MP3, etc.)
- **Label** - Filter by record label

## Sorting

Sort your view by:

- **Recently added** - Newest imports first
- **Artist** - Alphabetical by artist
- **Album** - Alphabetical by album title
- **Year** - Chronological by release year
